Gather your details

This is a good moment to gather the details you want to share.

  • what happened before, during, and after the event
  • medicines you take now
  • questions you want answered

04 / Meet The Doctor

Meet your seizure doctor

An epileptologist is a neurologist who specializes in seizures. The visit is a conversation, not a test you can fail.

05 / Your Story

06 / The Full Story

The full story helps

Your doctor is piecing together more than one moment. The event itself, the history around it, and your daily life and support system can all help explain what happened.

07 / Understanding Epilepsy

One seizure does not automatically mean epilepsy

A seizure is a sudden burst of electrical activity in the brain. Having one seizure does not automatically mean epilepsy. This visit is about understanding what happened and what still needs to be checked next.

Bring Your Questions

Don't be afraid to ask.

  • Can I drive?
  • What should I do if this happens again?
  • Do I need testing right away?
  • When should I call the clinic?
  • What should my family watch for?
Write your questions down before the visit so you don't forget.
